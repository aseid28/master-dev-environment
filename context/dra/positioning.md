# DRA — Positioning

What makes us different, in three layers.

## 1. Holistic go-to-market
Go-to-market is holistic: you can't win by advertising well alone — you also need the RevOps system that supports the pipeline engine, a digital experience that actually converts, and a brand/content strategy that says things the audience cares about. The core thesis: **siloing these is usually what's causing the client's problem**, and most agencies are themselves siloed. We sell the integration. See [[frameworks#selling-models]].

## 2. Personality: friendship + honesty
The brand differentiator is **community** — "friendship is the go-to-market," "friendship-based go-to-market." And **honesty** — the motto is **"build trust first."** This is the answer to the **mechanic problem**: clients can't evaluate technical GTM work any more than they can evaluate a $3,000 car repair, so they buy on trust. Treat people honestly and fairly and the trust becomes the product. See [[frameworks]].

## 3. Niche + proprietary product
Deep niches: **B2B technology**, especially **cybersecurity / AI** (some CX). And the **maturity-model Roadmap** is genuinely differentiated in our space — a proprietary diagnostic that grades a client's GTM and right-sizes what comes next. See [[dra/pillars-services]].
