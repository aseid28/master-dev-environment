# Digital Reach (DRA) — Index

A boutique, elite, **B2B-technology go-to-market agency** — pipeline generation for B2B tech, weighted toward **security, cybersecurity, and AI**. I'm **co-founder and COO**.

## What we actually do
Three core service areas, holistic by design (you can't silo them — see [[dra/positioning]]):
1. **Pipeline generation** — paid media, SEO/AEO (incl. LLM search), content syndication.
2. **Digital experience** — websites, ad creative, design, dev.
3. **RevOps** — the data systems and intelligence behind spend, reporting, and enabling sales/marketing (increasingly: enabling clients' AI systems).

**Flagship product:** the **Go-to-Market Roadmap** — we grade a client against our proprietary maturity model across the four pillars / "GTM essentials," then hand them a prioritized, costed, timelined plan. It right-sizes the engagement and opens the door to bigger work. ~$5k, 2–4 weeks, money-back. Splittable per-pillar.

## My role (COO)
I sit above **everything**: client services (CX), operations (biz/people/finance/security), and growth (sales + marketing). Day-to-day, less of the strategic work I want and more firefighting — leveling up CX for upmarket accounts we're not quite built for, plus a lot of selling and owning key relationships. The whole point of [[goals-projects|the AI build]] is to get me out of the low-leverage parts.

**What I want to keep:** managing/mentoring leaders, business-improvement strategy, crisis triage, financial strategy, closing hard sales, a few high-value relationships, podcasts/video, and managing co-founders Ben & Zach.
**What I want to delegate:** project/task prioritization + follow-up, marketing content + social, financial modeling/QA (the "NiCE PO Sheet"), difficult client relationships, ABM/RevOps depth, event kickoff, and new-tech/AI exploration.
