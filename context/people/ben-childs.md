# Ben Childs

Co-founder and **President of DRA**; a friend since elementary school. The relationship I've kept healthiest *personally* while it's hardest *professionally* — and right now the most depleting work relationship I have.

## Origin
Met in elementary school in Lake Oswego; bonded over basement poker games in high school. (I was a bit of a bully toward him as a little kid — possible origin of some dynamic.) Long friendship through college (he was at Santa Clara). He started **Digital Reach** as a sole proprietor (~$3k/mo, cold-called ad sales, satisfied to coast) while we were SF roommates; a trip to Peru is where we really talked it into a business. Brought in Zach, reincorporated, and it became real. See [[dra/index]].

## Current state — *restricted*
- **On a performance plan.** Salary downgraded to a more commission/performance basis. Has chronically missed sales targets.
- **No longer the majority shareholder** after the 2024–25 restructure; Zach and I can choose to terminate his employment, and we're close to it. See [[dra/financials-equity]].
- Has a **form of epilepsy** and some health issues he keeps very close.

## What works / what's hard
- **Works:** takes feedback genuinely well; strong overall vision for where the business should go; good moral compass; great in-person with prospects.
- **Hard:** not organized, struggles to work consistently and follow through, keeps things close to the vest, doesn't ask for help. The pattern: I confront it, he agrees, nothing changes. See [[failure-modes]].
- We've largely succeeded at **separating work from friendship** — better than Zach managed.

## Working norms (per PA briefing)
Anything sales-related → **Ben + Ashley**. His highest-value mode is in-person: get him onto planes, into conferences and events, building the network. Based in **Lake Oswego, OR**.
