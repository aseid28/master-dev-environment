# Bio & Timeline

The short version: born into an entrepreneurial, money-stressed-but-not-poor household; sailed through school; made a living at online poker before I had a real job; spent my early 20s chasing happiness around the world before figuring out it travels with you; landed in San Francisco, started DRA, found my people at Burning Man, found Clara, and ended up in Brooklyn running an agency and trying to build my way out of the parts of it I don't love.

## The arc
- **1987-08-03** — Born, Palo Alto CA. Dad was finishing a Stanford PhD. *(Birth city is canonical per briefing; transcript garbled it.)*
- **~1990** — Family moves to **Lake Oswego, OR** (affluent, white, safe Portland suburb) to be near Mom's parents. Traditional Americana upbringing; felt a little limited there.
- **Teens** — Serious football player. Also the hardest stretch of my life: depression, suicidal as a 13–15-yr-old, near-hospitalization. Came out the other side and haven't been truly depressed since ~16–17. *(restricted — see [[body-health#mental-health]])*
- **2005** — Graduates Lake Oswego HS, ~#3 in class. Easy student; spread across sports, music, politics, volunteering.
- **2005–2009** — **Dartmouth** (family legacy: both brothers, dad, uncle, grandfather). Started in government + Arabic; dropped gov once poker took off. Study-abroad in **Morocco (2007)**. Graduated **Middle Eastern Studies**.
- **2006–2013** — **Professional online poker.** Among the regularly-winning high-stakes players on Full Tilt / PokerStars. Wrote *Easy Game: Making Sense of No-Limit Hold'em* (2009). This is where the real worldview formed — see [[frameworks]].
- **~2009–2012** — Post-college wandering: **Buenos Aires → Bangkok → (China trip) → Portland.** Had money and total freedom; was still sad. "Everywhere I went, there I was."
- **2011-04-15** — **Poker's Black Friday.** Federal govt shuts down US online poker, freezes accounts. Re-released the book to raise cash; played from abroad where possible.
- **2012** — Moves to **San Francisco.** Where things turned — got happy. Roommates with Ben Childs.
- **2013** — DRA reincorporates from Ben's sole proprietorship into **Digital Reach Agency, LLC**; I come in as a part-time partner (~9%). See [[dra/index]].
- **2013–2014** — Stints in **Paris**; SF stays home base.
- **~2015** — Full-time at DRA.
- **2016** — Pivot year. Cut ties with three exes around my 29th birthday; got an impromptu invite to **Burning Man**, which I'd thought was dumb. Went, met basically all my current best friends. See [[burning-man]].
- **2016** — Grandfather **"Papa"** (Mom's father, taught me piano) passes.
- **2017** — Co-founds Burning Man camp **IQE (In Queso Emergency)**.
- **2018-10-04** — Meets **Clara Merlino** in Seattle, ~a month after the worst breakup. See [[people/clara-merlino]].
- **2020** — Builds the Vanlife van with Clara during COVID (~90% done). Leaves SF.
- **~2020–2021** — Seattle area during COVID.
- **2022–2023** — Moves to **New York / Williamsburg**. Williamsburg = SF/NY hybrid; slower than Manhattan, great climbing gym, the L into the city.
- **2025-07-05** — **Marries Clara.**
- **2026** — Now: Brooklyn (172 N 10th St 3A) with Clara and roommate Scott. Running DRA as COO, building the AI dev environment this wiki feeds. See [[goals-projects]].
