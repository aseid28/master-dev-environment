# Burning Man / IQE

The biggest community in my life and a core identity. "The closest thing to a utopian experiment I know" — radically non-judgmental, artistic, full of love, music, food, and my people. It runs on the same value that runs everything else: [[values#community]].

## My history with it
- **2016** — First year, on an impromptu invite right after the worst breakup of my life (around my 29th birthday). I'd thought it was dumb. Instead I met basically all my current best friends and fell into a two-year relationship. Mind-blowing.
- **2017** — A group of us split off and built our own camp, **IQE (In Queso Emergency)** — a Herculean labor of love (warehouse in Vegas, tons of money and manual labor). Originally a poker-player crew.
- **2018** — The hard year: my then-girlfriend was injured in an accident, broke up with me in front of everyone, and left. I left early, swore I was done. A month later I met Clara.
- **2019** — Brought Clara; a fabulous year.
- **2020–2021** — Canceled (COVID).
- **2022–2024** — Back and great.
- **2025** — Rough — rain, camp damaged in a storm.
- **2026** — **Taking the year off.** Back in **2027**.

## The camp
~35 people/year. Communal, fun-loving, non-judgmental, mutually supportive. I'm one of the two core leaders (with **Nick Binger**), doing orientation, recruiting, logistics, and the spreadsheets. Co-organized with **Nick Binger, Stephanie/Steph Stern, and Dan Bookstaber**.

## Cadence
A mid-year prep trip to **Reno** (Apr–Jul), then the event itself ~**Aug 20 → ~Sep 2**. See [[routines-preferences]] for the broader annual trip rhythm.
