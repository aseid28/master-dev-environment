# Body & Health

Tracked obsessively; mostly in good shape with a few chronic constraints.

## Training split
Alternates every other day. Core lifts are 3×5 unless noted.
- **Day A** — Squat 3×5, Bench 3×5, weighted Pull-ups 3×5, Bicep curls 3×8, Farmer's carries, full PT set.
- **Day B** — Shoulder press 3×5, Romanian deadlift 3×5, Tricep press 3×10, Cable rows 3×5, Farmer's carries, full PT set.
- **PT (every session)** — Achilles: eccentric heel drops, calf raises, weighted calf drops. Back: bird dogs, dead bugs, side planks, the McGill Big Three, abductions.
- **Cardio days** — 30–60 min, mostly elliptical + bouldering, some rowing. Jogging is out while the Achilles is flaring.

**Current working weights:** Bench ~185 · Squat ~200 · RDL ~365 · Shoulder press 65–70 · Rows ~170 · Weighted pull-ups +45 (belt) · Farmer's carry 65 · Curls 40 · Tricep press 120–130.
