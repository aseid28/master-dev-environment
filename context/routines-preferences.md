# Routines & Preferences

## Daily shape
- **Wake 7:30–8:30** (8 Sleep alarm window 7:45–8:15, vibration + bed-warming + cracked curtains). Clothes/bag laid out the night before.
- Kitchen: creatine + atorvastatin, coffee into a Contigo thermos.
- **Walk ~10 min to Vital Climbing Gym** (Williamsburg). Sip coffee, do **triage** (~1–1.5 hr): habit tracker, email, batched Slack, texts, Asana intake.
- **Workout** (~10am–12pm). Lift M/W/F, cardio T/Th/Sa.
- **~12–1pm** — home, shower, light lunch (bean salad + fish/shrimp, or a protein shake).
- **~1–6pm** — calls, near-continuous, few breaks. Mornings are protected for deep work + gym; only sales/high-value client calls get in before ~noon.
- **Evening** — cook or go out; fiction on Kindle ~20–30 min; phone charges *outside* the bedroom from ~9:30. **Bed ~10:30–11** (later, 12–2am, on weekends).
- **Weekends** — same skeleton minus the afternoon call-block; park, coffee-shop deep work, cooking, friends. Sleeps in later.

## Diet
**Pescatarian** — no beef/pork/chicken bought for the house (chicken is a rare treat). Protein = fish, shrimp, tofu, eggs, protein powder, lots of beans. Skips breakfast (coffee is breakfast); small lunch (300–500 cal); robust dinner, loves cooking (French/peasant stews, curries, stir-fries, tagine). Snacking when bored is the weak point (see [[failure-modes]]). Only texture he hates: mushy → "flan." Full grocery-staples list lives in the PA briefing.

## Travel rules
- **One-way tickets always**, even round trips.
- **Direct** basically always (will pay +$100–200 to avoid a stop).
- **No red-eyes**, avoid pre-7:45am departures; if a flight is before 7:45am, pre-book an Uber/Lyft.
- **Coach, cheapest fare**, airline-agnostic. Window on night flights, aisle on day flights; won't usually pay to pick seats.
- **Pack light** — backpack/carry-on, never checks.
- **NYC airports: LGA > JFK; never EWR** unless forced. (Long-haul/west-coast usually forces JFK.)
- De-facto airlines by hub: Delta/JetBlue from NYC, United from SF, Alaska from Seattle.
- ~1–2 trips/month.
